Save money on prescriptions

Prepaying is the easy way to save money on all your NHS prescriptions.

You can pay a set price for 3 or 12 months. This saves you money if you need more than:

  • 3 prescriptions in 3 months
  • 12 prescriptions in 12 months

The more prescriptions you need, the more you save.

What you can buy

A prescription costs £8.60 per item, but a prescription prepayment costs:

  • £29.10 for 3 months
  • £104 for 12 months (or 10 Direct Debit instalments of £10.40)

How it works

Buy your prescription prepayment online. We give you a prepayment number, start and end date.

Tick box F on the back of your prescription and take it to your pharmacy. They will give you your prescription without charge.

Save time – do it online

Buying online is quick and easy. You can:

  • start your prescription prepayment today
  • set it to start in the next month - ready for your next pharmacy visit
  • backdate it for the past month - see Prescription refunds below

Buy now

 

Prescription refunds

You can save money on prescriptions you’ve bought in the past month. This is only if you picked up a refund form (FP57) from your pharmacy at the time.

When you buy prepayment today, backdate your cover on our online form (link). Then take your refund form and proof of prepayment to your pharmacy.

Free prescriptions

If you are unsure whether you qualify for free prescriptions, please visit our guide.

Other ways to buy

By post

To apply by post, complete the application form (FP95). This is available from pharmacies and doctors surgeries.

Download the FP95 application form

You can pay by cheque or postal order made payable to ‘NHS Business Services Authority’. Alternatively, you can set up a Direct Debit instruction if you’re applying for 12 months’ prepayment.

When you have completed and signed the form, send it to:

PPC
NHS Business Services Authority
152 Pilgrim Street
Newcastle Upon Tyne
NE1 6SN

We aim to process postal applications within 3 working days of receiving the form.

Your prepayment will start from the date we receive your application. That is unless you ask for a different start date. This can be up to one month earlier or later than the date we receive your application.

Over the phone

If you want to apply for a prepayment over the phone, call us on 0300 330 1341.

Please have your:

  • debit or credit card details if you want to pay in full
  • your bank or building society account details to pay for 12 months by Direct Debit

Your prepayment will start from the date we receive your phone call. That is unless you ask for a different start date.  This can be up to one month earlier or later than the date of your phone call. From a pharmacy registered to sell prepayment

From a pharmacy registered to sell PPCs

You will need to complete an FP95 application form (available from the pharmacy) and pay at the till. 

The option to pay for a 12 month prepayment certificate by 10 monthly Direct Debit instalments is not available within pharmacies.

The pharmacy will forward your details to us and you will normally receive your PPC within 7 days of us receiving your application. 

Your prepayment will start from the date of purchase, unless you ask for a different start date.  This can be up to one month earlier or later than the date you buy your prepayment.
