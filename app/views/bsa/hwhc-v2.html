Save money on NHS prescriptions

Prepaying is the easy way to save money if you regularly pay for NHS prescriptions.

You can pay a set price for 3 or 12 months, no matter how many prescriptions you need.

This saves you money if you need more than:

  • 3 prescriptions in 3 months
  • 12 prescriptions in 12 months

The more prescriptions you need, the more you save.

How much you pay

A prescription costs £8.60 per item, but prepaying for an unlimited number of prescriptions is only:

  • £29.10 for 3 months
  • £104 for 12 months (or 10 Direct Debit instalments of £10.40)

How it works

Prepay for your prescriptions online. We give you prepayment details at the end to use to collect prescriptions.

Tick box 'F' on the back of your prescription and take it to your pharmacy. They will ask for your prepayment details, which you should give them.

They will give you your prescription without charge.

Before you start

Prepaying online is quick and easy. You can:

  • start prepaying today
  • set it to start in the next month - ready for your next pharmacy visit
  • backdate it for the past month - see Prescription refunds below

Buy now

Prescription refunds

You can save money on prescriptions you’ve bought in the past month. This is only if you picked up a refund form (FP57) from your pharmacy at the time.

When you prepay today, backdate your cover on our online form. Then take your refund form and prepayment details to your pharmacy.

Free prescriptions

If you are unsure whether you qualify for free prescriptions, please visit our guide.

Other ways to buy

Over the phone

If you want to prepay over the phone, call us on 0300 330 1341.

Please have your:

  • debit or credit card details if you want to pay in full
  • your bank or building society account details to pay for 12 months by Direct Debit

Your prepayment will start from the date we receive your phone call. That is unless you ask for a different start date. This can be up to one month earlier or later than the date of your phone call.

From a pharmacy registered to sell prescription prepayments

Prepay for prescriptions with cash or debit/credit card at registered pharmacies.

The option to pay for 12 months by 10 monthly Direct Debit instalments is not available.

The pharmacy will fill in an online form and give you your prepayment number straight away.

Your prepayment will start from the date of prepayment, unless you ask for a different start date.

This can be up to one month earlier or later than the date of prepayment.
