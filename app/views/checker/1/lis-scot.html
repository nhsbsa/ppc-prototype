There are two ways you could get help with your health costs

Apply for help to pay for NHS costs

If you find it hard to pay your NHS costs, you could get help if your left with less than £128 after you pay your council tax and housing costs.

How much help you get depends on your household income and outgoings.

You don't have to be claiming benefits to apply.

Any help you're entitled to is also available to your partner.

Find out more

Buy a prescription prepayment certificate

A prescription prepayment certificate (PPC) could save you money if you regularly get prescriptions.

You can buy a PPC to cover the cost of all your prescriptions for 3 or 12 months.

One prescription costs £8.40 but a PPC costs:

  • £29.10 for 3 month
  • £104 for 12 months
