You get free prescriptions

Because you live in wales, NHS prescriptions are free as long as you have:

  • a GP in Wales and
  • the prescription is dispensed by a pharmacist in Wales.

If you have a GP in England

If you live in Wales but your GP practice is in England, you are still able to get free prescriptions.

You will need to show the pharmacist a valid entitlement card along with your prescription.

How to apply for an entitlement card

Your entitlement card should be sent to you automatically, but if not contact the Shared Services Partnership.
