Based on what you've told us:
You get free prescriptions and NHS sight tests

Because you're over 60, you get free:

  • NHS prescriptions
  • NHS sight tests

You don't get help towards the cost of:

  • NHS dental check ups and treatment
  • New or replacement glasses or contact lenses
  • NHS wigs and fabric supports
  • Travel to receive NHS treatment

Proving you don't pay

Prescriptions

To get free prescriptions tick box C, (third box down) on the back of the prescription form.

If you find it hard to pay for the other things you don't get free, you might get help through the Low Income Scheme.
