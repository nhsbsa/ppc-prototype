What type of Jobseeker's Allowance (JSA) do you get?

There are 2 types of JSA, income-based and contributions-based.

Where to find out your JSA type

Your Jobseeker's Allowance letter

The type of JSA you get is shown on page 3 or 4, under the heading ‘How your Jobseeker's Allowance was worked out’. It will say either:

  • 'We call this Income-based Jobseeker's Allowance, or
  • 'We call this contribution-based'Jobseeker's Allowance.

Find out more about the different types of ESA.

If you're still unsure contact the Jobcentre Plus Helpline.
