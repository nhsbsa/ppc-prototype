Check what help you could get to pay for NHS costs

Although most NHS treatment is free, there is normally a charge for:

  • NHS prescriptions
  • NHS dental check ups and treatment
  • sight tests
  • new, replacement or repairing glasses and contact lenses
  • NHS wigs
  • fabric supports- things like surgical bras, support tights and spinal support
  • travelling for NHS treatment

You could be entitled to help with these costs depending on your circumstances.
