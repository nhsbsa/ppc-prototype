Privacy notice

The NHS Business Services Authority will process the information supplied by you.

This is for the purposes of managing your payment for prescription prepayment.

Your personal information

We have gained information from you. We will not share this information with other organisations or individuals.

Contacting you

We may contact you, by any method you have provided, to discuss:

  • your prepayment purchase
  • our quality of service to you

Retaining your personal information

We will delete your personal data from our systems and files no later than 7 years after you buy prepayment.

You are required to provide information to us for statutory/contractual reasons.

Consent

You consent to the disclosure of relevant information to the NHSBSA.

Cookies

When we provide services to you, we want to make them easy, useful and reliable.

Where we deliver online services, it involves placing small files on your device. These are cookies

Most web browsers allow some control of most cookies through the browser settings.

To find out more about cookies, visit www.allaboutcookies.org

It gives you information on how to:

  • see what cookies have been set
  • manage and delete them

For the prescription prepayment service, we use the cookie: JSESSIONID.

This is essential for the service to work and stays on your device until you close your web browser.

Further details of the other cookies and analytics used on our websites, visit:

https://www.nhsbsa.nhs.uk/our-policies/privacy

Your rights

We will not transfer your information outside the UK.

We manage the information you provide as required by Data Protection law. This includes the General Data Protection Regulations (GDPR).

You have the right to:

  • receive a copy of the information NHSBSA hold about you
  • request changes to your information if you believe it was not correct at the time you provided it
  • request deletion of your information if you believe we have it for longer than necessary

Details of NHSBSA processing appear on our website at:

https://www.nhsbsa.nhs.uk/our-policies/data-protection
